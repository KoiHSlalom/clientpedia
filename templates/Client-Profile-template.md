## Client Profile — Template

Purpose: capture canonical client information for quick lookup and context.

Fields (required)
- Client ID
- Name
- Industry
- HQ (city, country)
- Primary Market Owner
- Status (Active / Dormant / Closed)

Fields (recommended)
- Logo (link)
- Size (revenue / employees)
- Parent / Affiliates
- Short summary (1–2 sentences)
- Key contacts (name — role — email — phone — last touch date)
- Current engagements (title — owner — dates — short outcome)
- Recent documents (title — type — link)
- Tags (services, tech, region)

Past Projects
- List major past projects with: title, brief summary, dates, owners, key outcomes, and links to postmortems or deliverables.

Upcoming Projects
- List known upcoming projects/opportunities: title, expected start, estimated duration, owners, and current stage (planning/approved/confirmed).

Notes
- Keep summary concise. Link to source documents and engagements rather than copying.

Example
- Client ID: C001
- Name: Acme Corp
- Industry: Retail
- HQ: Chicago, USA
- Primary Market Owner: Jane Doe
